## About Us

KERONE Engineering Solutions Ltd. is a Mumbai-based industrial machinery manufacturer with nearly **five decades of engineering excellence**. What began in 1976 as a proprietorship firm providing heating solutions has grown into a globally recognised manufacturer and exporter of high-end process heating, drying, coating, and sterilisation systems.

In 1990, KERONE diversified into advanced heating and drying technologies through strategic collaborations with world-class consultants and industry leaders — pioneering application and implementation engineering across sectors.
